Practice Areas

Comprehensive legal strategies for complex challenges across multiple domains of law.

Civil Litigation

Dealing with disputes between individuals or organisations. We handle property disputes, breach of contract, personal injury, and employment matters with strategic precision.

Criminal Defense

Providing robust legal defense in criminal prosecutions. We protect your rights at every stage—from police custody and bail to investigations and court trials.

Property Law

Specialised advisory in real estate transactions, title verifications, redevelopment agreements, and litigation for property encroachment or ownership disputes.

Family Matters

Compassionate and effective legal help in divorce cases, child custody disputes, maintenance, and distribution of ancestral properties under Hindu and Special Marriage Acts.

Corporate Law

Advising businesses on legal structures, compliance, commercial contracts, and handling corporate litigations. We act as strategic legal partners for local businesses.

Legal Consultancy

Strategic legal advice to prevent disputes before they arise. We provide clarity on complex legal documentations, regulatory requirements, and risk mitigation.

Our Specialised Services

High Court Representation

Advocate Arvind R. Singh has vast experience in representing clients before the Bombay High Court. Our expertise covers Writ Petitions, Appeals, and original side litigations. We ensure that our clients' cases are presented with the highest level of legal craftsmanship.

Matrimonial Disputes & Family Law

We understand the emotional sensitivity of family law matters. Our approach blends compassion with firm legal strategy to resolve disputes related to divorce, maintenance, domestic violence, and child custody, always striving for the most amicable resolution possible.
